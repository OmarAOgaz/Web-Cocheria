How We Can Help

At Cochería San Marcos, we understand that this is a difficult time for families. Our support services are designed to assist you with the necessary arrangements and provide guidance throughout the process.

Available Support Services

  • Emotional Support: Our trained staff is here to listen and provide comfort.
  • Planning Assistance: We help you plan the details of the service to honor your loved one.
  • Resource Guidance: Access to community resources and support groups.
  • Aftercare Services: Follow-up support to help you through the grieving process.
